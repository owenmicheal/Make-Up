BUGEMA UNIVERSITY

Bugema University is a private, co-educational Ugandan university affiliated
with the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
It is a part of the Seventh-day Adventist education system,
the world's second largest Seventh-day Adventist University.

The following are the different schools in Bugema University

  • 1 Bugema University School of Graduate Studies
  • 2 Bugema University School of Computing and Informatics
  • 3 Bugema University School of Business
  • 4 Bugema University School of Natural Sciences
  • 5 Bugema University School of Health Sciences
  • 6 Bugema University School of Theological Studies
  • 7 Bugema University School of Education
  • 8 Bugema University School of Social Sciences
  • 9 Bugema University Institute of Professional Growth
  • 10 Bugema University Intensive English Program
